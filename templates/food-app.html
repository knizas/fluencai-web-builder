Recipe Details

Sun-kissed Lemon Pasta

A bright, creamy pasta with fresh lemon, garlic, and parsley. Perfect for a weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti or linguine
  • 1 ripe avocado (for creaminess) or 2 tbsp cream
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cook pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Whisk avocado/cream with lemon juice, zest, garlic, and olive oil until smooth.
  3. Drain pasta and toss with sauce. Add parsley and seasoning.
  4. Serve warm with extra lemon wedges.

Nutritional Information

Calories: ~420
Protein: ~14g
Carbs: ~54g
Fat: ~16g
